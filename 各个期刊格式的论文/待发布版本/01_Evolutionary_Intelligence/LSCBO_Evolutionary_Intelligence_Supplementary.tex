\section*{S3. Aggregate Statistical Outputs Used in the Main Manuscript}
Section 5.1 of the main manuscript reports rank-based statistics computed from 30 paired runs on the full 29-function set. The comparison contains nine algorithms and 7,830 completed runs. Pairwise post-hoc tests are performed on the 29 per-function mean errors rather than by pooling all 870 function--run observations, so the supplementary summary follows the same cross-function protocol as the main text.

The Nemenyi critical difference is approximately 2.23. The rank gaps from LSCBO to SA (0.97) and PSO (1.66) are below this threshold. The pairwise Wilcoxon--Holm and all-pairs Nemenyi procedures answer different post-hoc questions; the main manuscript therefore reports the exact pairwise outcomes and avoids the claim that LSCBO is significantly superior to every comparator.

\section*{S4. Availability Note for the Benchmark Materials}
The reproducibility package contains all 7,830 per-run rows, per-function descriptive statistics, convergence checkpoints, the complete rank table, paired statistical outputs, the locked parameter record, and the official-function provenance information. This supplement is limited to benchmark coverage and statistical protocol; the analytical scheduling study and its 12-row CloudSim Plus smoke replay are reported separately in Section 5.2 of the main manuscript.
